Fourstroke 01 LTD

Description:

Fourstroke 01 is an XCO performance bike that exceeds the demands of the world’s top racers. On a mission to set the pace with our unique Autodrop
technology and unrivalled APS dual-link suspension with 100mm of front and rear travel, it bests the competition in a Premium Carbon frame. This
revolutionized racing machine arrives with integrated protection and is designed around the progressive BWC geometry, now accommodating two water bottles
in the front triangle for long-distance missions. Fourstroke 01 LTD is a medal winning machine with the innovative SRAM XX SL Eagle Transmission drivetrain,
Fox Float 34 SC Factory fork and Fox Float DPS EVOL LV Factory shock, DT Swiss XRC 1200 wheels and the BMC Autodrop integrated dropper seatpost.
